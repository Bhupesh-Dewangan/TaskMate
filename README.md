# TaskMate — Simple To-Do List App

TaskMate is a lightweight and easy-to-use to-do list app that helps you keep track of your daily tasks without any distractions. It’s designed for people who just want a clean, straightforward way to stay organized.
## Features
- Add Tasks – Quickly add new tasks to your list
- Delete Tasks – Remove tasks you no longer need
- Mark as Complete – Check off tasks when they’re done
- Local Storage – Your tasks are saved in your browser, so they’re still there when you come back
- 
## Why TaskMate?
TaskMate focuses on the basics — no complicated tools, no clutter. Just a simple and reliable way to manage your daily tasks directly in your browser.Stay organized. Stay productive. Keep it simple with TaskMate.
